## Overview

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Seoul National University, advised by
[Prof. Kyoung Mu Lee](https://cv.snu.ac.kr/).
My research interests lie in machine learning and computer vision, particularly in
3D human understanding and image restoration. I aim to connect high-level semantic
reasoning with low-level visual reconstruction.

> **Research focus.** Building robust visual systems that understand people and scenes
> while recovering reliable visual details from imperfect observations.

**01 — 3D HUMAN**  \
Human pose, shape, and hand reconstruction under challenging visual conditions.

**02 — RESTORATION**  \
Generative image restoration, super-resolution, and degradation-aware modeling.

**03 — VISION + LEARNING**  \
Learning-based systems that combine semantic understanding with visual reconstruction.
