## Overview

Hands frequently exhibit severe motion blur because of their fast and
articulated movement. This makes 3D hand recovery difficult: a single blurry
image can contain visual evidence from multiple time steps and may not reveal
the precise hand configuration at a specific instant.

> **EBH** provides real blurry hand images together with corresponding event
> streams and 3D hand annotations, enabling the study of temporally ambiguous
> hand motion in realistic capture conditions.
